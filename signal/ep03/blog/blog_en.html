Palantir sits between Early Adopters and the Chasm. It has secured government and enterprise customers, but hasn't yet crossed over into SMB mass-market territory.

Chasm ① SMB Penetration
AIP bootcamps are expanding, but SMB conversion rates remain unproven. Losing one enterprise client requires dozens of SMBs to compensate.
Chasm ② AI-Native Competition
LLM-native startups are beginning to solve data integration without Ontology. 2027–2028 is the inflection point to watch.

🐻 Bear Signal Check — What's Actually Happening Now

✅

Azure Secret and Top Secret expansion is actively happening. Palantir's security clearance moat is under real pressure.
✅
DOGE Cuts Active — In Progress
DOGE-driven federal budget cuts are being executed. However, Palantir appears protected as core national security infrastructure, limiting actual damage so far.
⏳
LLM Replaces Ontology — 2027–28 Risk Not Yet Reached

The technical combination is feasible, but there are no confirmed government agency migrations yet. Still in pilot territory.
