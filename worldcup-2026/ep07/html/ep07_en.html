VARIABLE 01

June 14 · MetLife, New York
All-time record: Spain 7W · Netherlands 4W · 4 draws

The 2010 Final rematch. The loser faces intense pressure in the race for second — a psychological blow that could ripple through the rest of the group.
VARIABLE 02
🇸🇳 Senegal's Upset Potential
The Qatar 2022 Memory
Senegal ran Netherlands close in Qatar 2022's group stage. If Ismaila Sarr is in top form, an upset against Netherlands or Switzerland is absolutely on the table — and would flip the entire group.
VARIABLE 03
🇨🇭 Switzerland's Silent Threat
The Spain Rivalry
Drew with Spain in 2022, lost to Spain in 2010. Switzerland are the most underrated team in World Cup history. Their defensive block + counter approach makes a 1-0 upset entirely realistic.
