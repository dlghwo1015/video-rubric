The Defining Moments
Chapters both legends wrote into World Cup history
2014 Brazil · Final
Messi's Tragedy and Triumph
Four goals, Golden Ball, a final appearance — then a 0-1 loss to Germany. Eight years later, 2022 Qatar: a shocking loss to Saudi Arabia, then seven goals in seven matches. He lifted the trophy at 35.
2006 Germany · 2018 Russia
Ronaldo's Two Peaks
2006 at age 21: led Portugal to their first-ever World Cup semifinal, finishing third. 2018: a solo hat-trick in a 3-3 draw against Spain — an individual moment of true GOAT-level quality.
2010 South Africa · The Zero
Messi's Worst Tournament
7 matches, 0 goals. Argentina crushed 0-4 by Germany. Yet this failure forged the hunger that drove 2014, and ultimately 2022.
2022 Qatar · Tearful Exit
Ronaldo's Last Chapter?
Came off the bench vs Switzerland in the Round of 16, then Portugal fell 0-1 to Morocco in the quarters. Tears on the pitch. Can a 41-year-old twist the ending in 2026?
