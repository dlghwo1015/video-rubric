🔵 France — 2018 Champions, 2022 Runners-Up

The deepest squad on the planet. Les Bleus are ready for revenge after the 2022 final heartbreak. Kylian Mbappé has grown into a superstar at Real Madrid and is already a legend after two World Cups. Eduardo Camavinga and Aurélien Tchouaméni control midfield, while Marcus Thuram adds attacking threat.

🌟 Key Player: Kylian Mbappé (Real Madrid · Age 26)   🎯 Midfield: Camavinga · Tchouaméni
🛡️ Attack: Marcus Thuram   ⚠️ Weakness: Defensive cohesion after GK change — but strengths vastly outweigh it

🔴 Portugal — Ronaldo's Final Chapter

Ronaldo's final World Cup stage. At 41, he's statistically in decline, but manager Roberto Martínez cannot leave him out. The real future is Rafael Leão — AC Milan's explosive winger. Bruno Fernandes drives the creativity, and Rúben Dias anchors the defense. Portugal's strategy combines Ronaldo's iconic presence with youthful energy.

⭐ Captain: Cristiano Ronaldo (41 · Final World Cup)   🚀 New gen: Rafael Leão (AC Milan) · Bruno Fernandes
🛡️ Defense: Rúben Dias   🎯 Strategy: Ronaldo icon status + Leão's explosive energy

🇲🇦 Morocco & 🇨🇦 Canada

Morocco returns as the team that created the 2022 Qatar miracle. Africa's first-ever semifinal — that history was no fluke. Coach Walid Regragui's defensive organization is world-class. Achraf Hakimi (PSG) and Sofyan Amrabat (Barcelona) are the key cogs. Their gameplan: 1-0 defensive football, dare you to break us down.

Canada, led by Alphonso Davies (Bayern Munich), are making history at their first ever home World Cup. The home crowd is a genuine factor. Against France and Portugal the walls are high, but the Morocco match is where Canada's tournament will be decided.

🔮 Three Key Variables in Group B

① June 19: France vs Portugal
One of the greatest group stage games ever. Neither side wants to finish second. The loser faces a psychological collapse that could cost them the whole tournament.
② The Ronaldo Effect
When Ronaldo scores, Portugal becomes a completely different team. When he doesn't score? The psychological pressure becomes immense and the team struggles to function.
③ Morocco's Defense
Even France struggled to beat Morocco in the 2022 semifinal. Regragui's defensive organization alone can manufacture an upset against any team in the world.
